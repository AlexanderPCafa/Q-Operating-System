What is Q OS

Since the dawn of computing and computational based interaction, we have had lots of different, incompatible mediums that worked against each other. Q OS is a project started by 13-year-old Raph Hennessy to try to build an Open-Source Operating System that would unify all hardware under a single OS that gave them connected power for a modern world. With Q OS, anything will be possible. For example, you could adjust the brightness of your bike light from your laptop, smart watch, smartphone, smart fridge or even smart car. You could even fly your remote controlled helicopter right from your landline phone.
